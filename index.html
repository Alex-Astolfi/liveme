Catalog Additional Info

This catalog was collected over the span of 10 days (from 01/10/24 to 10/10/24) in the context of the Studies On Human Behaviour course at the University of Trento. It consists of time diaries (one every 30 minutes + morning and evening questions) and data from my phone sensors, all collected through the iLog application. The data was then used to implement small statistical models, to understand factors impacting mood, mood variation and answer delay in time diaries.
